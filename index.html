Charlie's website

My ongoing projects. If you find something interesting, leave a message here.

Resume

A one-page resume built in LaTeX using several templates under Creative Commons 3.0 and exported to HTML with pdf2htmlEX. Best viewed on a larger screen.

Posters

A collection of conference abstracts and posters. Use the gear icon to toggle between documents without losing your reading position. Best viewed on a larger screen.

Machine learning in game theory

A database of two-player games and a convolutional neural network version of "GameNet" that predicts the players' choices. You are welcome to play with the model as well as contribute to the database.

Strategy in a month-long prisoner's dilemma

A database of games from a recent paper that had players engaged in a month-long prisoner's dilemma. I show how to process the data and attempt a simple analysis of which strategies worked best.

Brahms

A piano performance of Brahms Capriccio in G Minor from 7 Fantasien (Op. 116, No. 3). Start the audio with the HTML5 audio player and scroll down to follow the music score.

Griffes

A piano performance of Griffes Scherzo from Fantasy Pieces (Op. 6, No. 3). Start the audio with the HTML5 audio player and scroll down to follow the music score.
